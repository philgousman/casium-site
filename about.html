Why we started Casium

“I really want to fix this. I want to change this.”

Priyanka Kulkarni, Founder & CEO

Priyanka spent a decade building foundational AI at Microsoft. She also lived the immigration process herself, moving from an L-1 to an H-1B to an EB-1 that took more than three months with a traditional law firm. Having seen it as both an immigrant and an employer, she knew the experience could be far better. She founded Casium to bring AI and licensed attorneys together, and shrink months of paperwork into days.
